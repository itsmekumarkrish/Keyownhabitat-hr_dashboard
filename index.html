The Reality of Renting

Are You Losing Money Every Month?

Millions of Indians are trapped in the rent cycle, paying lakhs over years and building zero equity.

Your monthly rent pays someone else's mortgage. Over 10 years you pay ₹48–₹76 Lakhs and walk away with nothing.

Zero Equity

What is HOAS?

Home Ownership Advisory Service: India's Missing Link Between Renters and Homeowners

KeyOwn Habitat's HOAS is not a property portal. It is not a broker. It is a financial advisory platform that prepares you to own a home by fixing your credit profile, structuring your finances, and matching you with RERA-approved developers and partner banks.

We are asset-light, advice-first, and entirely on your side.

Financial Readiness

We assess your CIBIL score, income, and debt profile to understand exactly where you stand.

Expert Guidance

We create a personalised roadmap to mortgage readiness, fixing documentation gaps and optimising your loan eligibility.

Trusted Support

We stay with you from first assessment to final key handover, including developer negotiation and bank loan facilitation.

How It Works

Your Journey to Home Ownership

We've broken down the complex process of buying a home into a clear, supportive journey. We're with you every step of the way.

Step 1

Free Assessment

Book a free consultation. Our HOAS advisor assesses your CIBIL score, income, existing EMIs, and documentation gaps.

Step 2

Personalised Roadmap

We create your custom credit and financial readiness plan. Pay a one-time advisory fee of ₹9,999 to activate your full roadmap.

Step 4

Loan Disbursement & Ownership

With your mortgage approved, the funds are disbursed directly to the developer. Your financial transition is complete, you officially exit the rent trap and begin building your own asset legacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Home Ownership Advisory System.

What is HOAS?

HOAS stands for Home Ownership Advisory Service. It is KeyOwn Habitat's flagship advisory platform that helps renters become homeowners through credit assessment, financial planning, and developer matching.

How much does HOAS cost?

We charge a one-time non-refundable advisory fee of ₹9,999 which covers your full financial assessment and personalised homeownership roadmap.

Do I need a perfect credit score to start?

No. We work with clients at all credit levels. Our job is to assess where you are and guide you to where you need to be for mortgage approval.

How long does it take to become home-ready?

It depends on your current financial profile. Some clients are ready immediately. Others may need 3–6 months of credit improvement before applying for a mortgage.

Which cities do you operate in?

We currently operate in Bengaluru. We are planning to expand to Hyderabad and Mumbai in 2027.

Are you RERA registered?

Yes. We operate in full compliance with RERA guidelines and exclusively list RERA-certified projects.

How do I invest in KeyOwn Habitat?

We are currently raising ₹25 Lakhs through a community investment round. Contact us on WhatsApp to receive the investor document and revenue share agreement.

Is my investment safe?

This is a revenue share agreement, not a fixed deposit. Returns depend on business performance. However our projected operating profit of ₹67L+ per month comfortably covers all investor payouts and principal repayment.
